About Us

Toban Law Firm - Attorneys and Legal Advisors - is the culmination of over six decades of experience in the legal field, both within and outside the Kingdom. Founded in 1979 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, by the late lawyer and legal counsel Mohammed Abdul Hamid Toban, the firm has rapidly expanded its reach and expertise to provide comprehensive legal services across the Kingdom. In 1991, under the leadership of lawyer and legal Advisor Sameh Mohamed Toban, a branch was opened in Jeddah, further solidifying the firm's presence and commitment to serving clients throughout Saudi Arabia.


Over time in 2006 , Toban Law Firm has transformed into a professional corporation, bringing together a team of highly skilled lawyers and assistants from diverse backgrounds and specializations. This diverse team enables the firm to deliver exceptional legal services with a collaborative spirit, enhanced by its extensive international legal experience and astute negotiation capabilities.

Our Vision

Guided by the Islamic Sharia law, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia places a profound responsibility on legal counsels, who serve not only their clients but also their nation and ultimately, God. This responsibility demands individuals of unwavering integrity, wisdom, discernment, and unwavering faith.


At Toban Law Firm, we are committed to promoting legal awareness among business leaders through our comprehensive services and consultations in all legal and regulatory domains. Our aim is to empower our clients to embark on their business ventures with confidence and security, both within the Kingdom and across the globe.

Our Clients

Toban Law Firm proudly serves a distinguished clients, encompassing a broad spectrum of leading Saudi companies and institutions operating in diverse sectors both within and beyond the Kingdom's borders. We also represent numerous foreign companies with commercial ties to Saudi Arabia, as well as a distinguished clientele of Saudi businessmen on an individual basis. Our unwavering commitment to excellence, dedication to our clients' interests, and unwavering integrity have earned us a reputation as a premier legal provider, fostering long-standing client relationships since our inception.

Our Services

With years of experience and a team of specialized lawyers and legal advisors, we provide a broad range of services and legal advice for commercial and civil matters in and outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.


Our firm's capabilities cover a comprehensive list of areas, designed to support our clients' diverse needs, including but not limited to the following

Our Services

  • Corporate and Joint Venture: Business entity selection, foreign investment regulation, formation and operation of corporations, and distributorship licensing.
  • Contracts: Drafting and reviewing trade, management, commercial agencies, leasing, insurance, employment, sale, partnership, investment, loans, contracting, and supply contracts.
  • Foreign Investment: Legal consultations and services for foreign investments under Saudi Foreign Capital Investment Law.
  • Licenses: Obtaining industrial licenses, feasibility studies, and customs exemption certificates.
  • Intellectual Property: Advice on obtaining, protecting, and exploiting IP rights; prosecuting and defending IP actions.
  • Labor: Assistance in labor negotiations and disputes, drafting employment contracts, and representation before labor committees/courts.
  • Litigation and Arbitration: Representation in all levels of courts and arbitration panels for commercial, civil, maritime, labor, administrative, Shariah, banking, and insurance cases.
  • Debt Collection: Enforcement of claims and legal actions against debtors.
  • Agency and Franchise: Due diligence for selecting local agents, negotiation of agency agreements, and commercial registration requirements.
  • Real Estate: Services and consultations on ownership transfer, lease contracts, construction, mortgage.
  • Liquidation and Insolvency: Corporate liquidation, creditor composition, and insolvency declaration.
  • Legacy: Inheritance management, division, and distribution in accordance with Islamic Sharia.
  • Maritime: Shipping industry services, ship documentation, cargo carriage, terminal operations, and claims handling.
